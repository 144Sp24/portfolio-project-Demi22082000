My projects

VR Escaperooms

I've made some escaperooms in the VR. I've done this for a school in the Netherlands so that the students can learn in a fun way. This was part of my internship.

Making a multimedia story

In Cooperation with the CBS I've worked in a group to make this story about 2 brothers in the farming industry. This to show the difference between normal farming and biological farming.

A museum concept

We worked in groups on this project. We started with the question of how we can make this museum more appealing to the public. We have figured out that interaction would create curiosity and making people more interested in the content. We’ve done this true giving the public the choice of which story they want to hear, through pushing buttons. The buttons will activate a hologram with the story outspoken.

Making video's and everything else

For my internship I’ve made video’s and so much more. The video’s were meant to explain which kind of directions of school you can choose. I also made some other video’s. I was also busy with making some logo’s and icons for them to use.

STEAM

We have practically investigated how STEAM education can be delivered using VR. We have shared the experience in a multimedia product. We have created a digital exhibition where visitors can see the works of the children.

Festival ideas

Designing a captivating festival concept to enlighten people about Maastricht's rich history. Integrating interactive exhibits, historical reenactments, and immersive storytelling, the event aims to engage and educate attendees, fostering a deeper connection with the city's cultural heritage. An exploration of Maastricht's past through a dynamic and entertaining festival experience.
